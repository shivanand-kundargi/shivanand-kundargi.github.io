About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County, advised by Dr. Tejas Gokhale. I also closely collaborate with Dr. Kowshik Thopalli. My work spans continual learning, interpretable AI, and machine unlearning focusing on harnessing concept-based interpretability for adapting to novelty and variations. I have published in CVPR-W, AAAI, and 3DV.

I am open for collaborations, reach out to me with to catchup and discuss on cool ideas.
